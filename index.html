Falls Group.

Translational bioinformatics, health informatics, and computational chemistry towards drug discovery and design and elucidation of adverse drug reactions and prediction of novel biomarkers and mecahnisms of action.

About Us

We are a data-centric computational group who leverage multiple heterogeneous datasets and libraries, e.g., chemicals, macromolecular targets, diseases/indications, electronic health record, and medicaid claims, all towards greater understanding of drug effects and mechanisms of action in a multiscale manner.
